Cereal

Description

Everyone's childhood favorite saturday morning breakfast -- cereal!

Enjoy everything from chocolate, corn flake, raisin bran and whole wheat

Try our personal favorite-- fruity pebbles! My favorite part is the cereal milk

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • Fruity Pebbles Cereal
  • Banana
  • Strawberry

Steps

  • Pour fruity pebbles into cereal bowl
  • Pour whole milk over fruity pebbles
  • Add cut strawberries and bananas to taste
  • Enjoy a good saturday morning cartoon with cereal
